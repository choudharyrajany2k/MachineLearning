what is autocorelation?

COrelation - the relationship between two variables in a time series data
Auto Corelation -  the relation ship between the values of variables at the present time and past time.

value at time t  -  p
value at time t-k  =  k

so the relationship between p and k is called as autocorelation.
The value of auto corelation is -1 and 1.
1 is perfect positive corelation  - completly corelated
-1 perfect negative corelation -  No relation

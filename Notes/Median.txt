whatis median?
	- the median is the value separating the higher half from the lower half of a data sample.
	- Can be considered as middle value of a distribution.
	- Most resistent statistics having breakdown point 50%
	
How to find median?
	- SOrt numbers in the distribution.
	- if odd then
			middle one the median
			e.g 1, 3, 3, 6, 7, 8, 9 
				The median is 6
	- if even then
			mean of two middle values 
			e.g 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9
				The median is (4+5)/2 = 4.5
